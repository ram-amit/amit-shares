Three early signals from the data

Directional findings to frame the discussion. Not conclusions.

1. Agentic traffic reaches Jax blind
315 Jax leads (90d) from AI/Agent landing pages. 0% opp conversion. Jax can't differentiate messaging because it only sees signup_prompt, not the LP or campaign.
2. Amanda meeting→opp is declining
61% (Jan) → 41% (Jun) → 26% (Jul partial). CM match doesn't explain it. Journey context may help qualification; the drop likely needs a separate RevAI ops look.
3. High-intent keywords convert well for Amanda
"monday crm" (116 leads): 63% meeting, 33% opp. If Amanda knew the keyword before the call, she could lean into CRM positioning earlier.

Open questions

Technical details intentionally light. These are the decisions we need from this group.

  • Where does context land in each agent? Jax plan "Strategic Intent"? Amanda pre-call brief? Oz L3 account context?
  • Lead-attached vs account-persisted? When an Amanda lead becomes an opp in a rep's portfolio, does Oz inherit the original marketing context?
  • Who builds the feed? MOPA Snowflake view → RevAI context table? Or dev touchpoint pipeline?
  • Is Amanda's meeting→opp drop a separate track? Journey context helps personalization; the decline may be qualification/routing.
